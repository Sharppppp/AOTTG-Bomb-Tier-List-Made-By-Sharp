How do I add a new Player?

Here you can see the criteria I use to add players.

Mechanical

Movement Speed: How quickly a player can move around the map.
Reaction Time: How fast a player can respond to threats and opportunities.

Stamina: Ability to sustain high performance over time.
Consistency: Reliability in maintaining performance throughout the game.

Accuracy: Precision in hitting targets.
Maneuverability: Skill in moving without crashing while attaking and positioning advantageously.
Timing: Proper timing of attacks.


Brain

Situational Awareness: Understanding the game environment and the positions of other players.
Decision Making: Ability to make quick and effective decisions under pressure.
Strategic Planning: Long-term planning and anticipating opponent moves.

Versatility: Ability to adapt to different situations and opponents.
Playstyle: Adaptability of a Player to play a specific playstyle (Sniper, Agressive, etc..)

Map Knowledge: Familiarity with the map layout.
Mechanics Understanding: Mastery of the game's mechanics and how to exploit them effectively.
